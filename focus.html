Frequently Asked Questions

Should I take FOCUS every day?
Yes! Unlike caffeine which builds tolerance and has diminishing returns, FOCUS shows positive adaptation effects. Your brain actually gets healthier with daily use — better sleep, better HRV, better recovery. This is the opposite of a crutch. It's genuine brain optimization.
How quickly will I feel it working?
Most people notice effects within 20-30 minutes. You'll experience sustained focus for 4-6 hours without the crash associated with caffeine. The flow state feeling — calm yet energized — is what you're looking for.
Is FOCUS really caffeine-free?
Yes. FOCUS uses paraxanthine (enfinity®), which is what caffeine becomes AFTER your liver metabolizes it and removes the junk. You get all the focus and energy benefits without the jitters, anxiety, or sleep disruption. It's the clean part of caffeine, isolated.
Will I build tolerance like with caffeine?
No — and this is the breakthrough. Studies show paraxanthine has NO habituation effect. In fact, we see the opposite: positive adaptation where your brain gets healthier over time. E-gaming athletes on paraxanthine improved week after week, while caffeine users declined.
Can I take FOCUS with coffee?
We recommend starting with FOCUS alone to experience its full benefits. Many users find they can reduce or eliminate their coffee intake entirely. If you do combine them, start with a lower dose of both and see how you feel.
What time should I take it?
Take FOCUS in the morning for daily brain health, or 20-30 minutes before you need peak concentration. Unlike caffeine, it won't wreck your sleep if taken in the morning — studies show it actually improves sleep quality. Avoid taking within 6 hours of bedtime.
