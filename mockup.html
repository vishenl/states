Product-Specific Questions

Will FOCUS make me jittery like coffee?
No. FOCUS uses paraxanthine instead of caffeine, which provides clean energy without the jitters, anxiety, or crash. The L-Theanine also promotes calm focus. Users describe it as "smooth, sustained energy" - alert and productive without feeling wired.
Will ZEN make me drowsy or sedated?
No. ZEN promotes calm without sedation - it's designed for daytime use. The ingredients (L-Theanine, Rhodiola, Lemon Balm) support your GABA system for relaxation while keeping you mentally sharp. It's perfect before stressful meetings or presentations.
How is BLISS different from antidepressants?
BLISS is not a medication and doesn't work like SSRIs. It uses natural compounds (Synapsa, Zembrin, Affron saffron) to support healthy serotonin function and reduce the brain's stress response. It elevates baseline mood and social openness without the side effects or dependency of pharmaceuticals. It's not a replacement for prescribed medications.
Does GAZM work for both men and women?
Yes! GAZM is formulated for all genders. The nitric oxide boosters (Nitrosigine, Beet Root) enhance blood flow universally. The adaptogens (Tongkat Ali, Ashwagandha) support healthy hormone balance in both men and women, promoting presence, connection, and physical response.
Will DREAM give me vivid or weird dreams?
DREAM contains Mugwort, traditionally used for enhancing dream vividness and recall. Many users report more memorable, vivid dreams - which can be valuable for creativity and subconscious processing. If you prefer dreamless sleep, you can take DREAM without the mugwort effect by taking it earlier (2 hours before bed).
