Sleep & Recovery

What is the optimal melatonin dose for sleep?
Research shows 0.3-0.5mg of melatonin is often more effective than higher doses (3-10mg). Most over-the-counter melatonin is drastically over-dosed. High doses can cause next-day grogginess, disrupt your body's natural melatonin production, and actually worsen sleep quality over time. The micro-dose approach (0.3-0.5mg) gently signals your brain that it's time to sleep without overwhelming your system. This mimics your body's natural melatonin release rather than flooding receptors.
What are the best natural sleep supplements besides melatonin?
The most effective natural sleep aids are Magnesium Glycinate, L-Theanine, Apigenin (chamomile extract), and Reishi mushroom. Magnesium Glycinate calms the nervous system and is the most bioavailable form for sleep. L-Theanine promotes relaxation without sedation. Apigenin binds to GABA receptors for gentle drowsiness. Reishi supports deep, restorative sleep phases. These work through different mechanisms than melatonin and can be combined for comprehensive sleep support without dependency.
Can supplements cause vivid dreams?
Yes, certain supplements enhance dream vividness and recall - this is generally a sign of deeper REM sleep. Mugwort (Artemisia vulgaris) has been used for centuries to promote lucid dreaming. Alpha-GPC and other choline sources can intensify dreams by supporting acetylcholine, which peaks during REM sleep. Vitamin B6 is also associated with dream recall. If you prefer less vivid dreams, take sleep supplements 2+ hours before bed rather than right at bedtime.
