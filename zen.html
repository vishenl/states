How quickly will I feel ZEN working?
Most people notice effects within 20-30 minutes of taking ZEN. You'll feel a wave of calm wash over you as the ingredients begin to work on your GABA receptors and stress response systems.
Will ZEN make me drowsy?
No. ZEN is designed for calm alertness, not sedation. You'll feel relaxed and centered while remaining mentally sharp. It's perfect for stressful workdays or before meditation.
Can I take ZEN with other supplements?
ZEN pairs well with other States products. Many users take FOCUS in the morning and ZEN in the afternoon or evening. Always consult your healthcare provider if you're on medications.
Is ZEN good for anxiety?
ZEN contains ingredients clinically shown to reduce anxiety and promote relaxation. It works through multiple pathways including GABA modulation and stress hormone reduction. However, it's not a replacement for medical treatment.
When is the best time to take ZEN?
Take ZEN whenever you need to de-stress: before meditation, during a hectic workday, or in the evening to unwind. Unlike sleep aids, it won't make you drowsy, so it's suitable for any time of day.
