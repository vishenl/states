Strategic Recommendations

1

Build "States Assessment" Quiz

Timeline: 4-6 weeks

Reduce decision paralysis and increase conversion by 20-30% with a personalized product recommendation quiz.

  • 3-4 questions: Primary challenge, when support needed, supplement experience
  • Personalized result: "Based on your answers, we recommend FOCUS"
  • Email capture: Before showing results for retargeting
  • Box Set fallback: For "not sure" visitors

Subscription-First Business Model

Timeline: 8-12 weeks

Shift from one-time sales to recurring revenue, increasing customer LTV by 5-10x.
