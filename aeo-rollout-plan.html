e.g., "Vishen" — include credentials, books, recognition
Core Method/Framework
The unique methodology taught (e.g., "Alpha & Theta brainwave meditation")

3 Universal FAQ Template (Per Quest)

Every Quest should have FAQs in these 6 categories. Below are the question templates — replace variables with Quest-specific content.

Category 1: Method & Science

// Captures educational searches, builds credibility

Q1: What is [METHOD_NAME] and does it actually work?
Q2: Is there scientific research behind [METHOD_NAME]?
Q3: How is [METHOD_NAME] different from [COMMON_ALTERNATIVE]?
Q4: Who created [METHOD_NAME] and what are their credentials?

Category 2: Results & Timeline

// Sets expectations, reduces refunds

Q1: How long does it take to see results from [METHOD_NAME]?
Q2: What results can I realistically expect from practicing [METHOD_NAME]?
Q3: Do the benefits of [METHOD_NAME] last long-term?
Q4: What do students typically achieve after completing [QUEST_NAME]?

Category 3: For Beginners

// Captures discovery traffic, addresses newcomer fears

Q1: Can beginners learn [METHOD_NAME] or do I need prior experience?
Q2: How much time per day do I need to practice [METHOD_NAME]?
Q3: What if I've tried [SIMILAR_METHOD] before and it didn't work for me?
Q4: Is [METHOD_NAME] suitable for [SPECIFIC_AUDIENCE]?

Category 4: Comparisons

// Intercepts competitive/comparison searches

Q1: What's the difference between [METHOD_NAME] and [COMPETITOR_METHOD]?
Q2: Is [METHOD_NAME] better than [ALTERNATIVE_APPROACH]?
Q3: Can I combine [METHOD_NAME] with [OTHER_PRACTICE]?
Q4: Why choose [QUEST_NAME] over free [METHOD_NAME] resources?

Category 5: Practical Use

// Reduces operational support tickets

Q1: How long is the [QUEST_NAME] program?
Q2: What's included in [QUEST_NAME]?
Q3: Can I access [QUEST_NAME] on mobile?
Q4: How long do I have access to the program?

Category 6: Safety & Concerns

// Addresses skepticism, builds trust

Q1: Is [METHOD_NAME] safe?
Q2: Are there any side effects from practicing [METHOD_NAME]?
Q3: Is [METHOD_NAME] compatible with [RELIGION/BELIEFS]?
Q4: What is Mindvalley's refund policy for [QUEST_NAME]?

Sample AEO Answer

Q: What is the Silva Method and does it actually work?

The Silva Method is a meditation and mental training system developed by José Silva in the 1960s that teaches you to access alpha and theta brainwave states for enhanced intuition, problem-solving, and stress reduction. Over 6 million people in 110+ countries have completed Silva training. Research from the University of Trinity Texas found participants showed significant improvements in memory, focus, and stress management. The method combines guided visualization, controlled relaxation, and mental exercises — it's not religious or mystical, but based on neuroscience principles of how brainwaves affect mental performance.

5 Rollout Process

1

Data Collection Sprint

Compile the required information for each Quest. Start with top 20 highest-traffic programs. Pull support ticket data, review existing sales page copy, and interview Quest managers.

Week 1-2
2

Search Intent Research

For each Quest, research what people actually search for. Use AnswerThePublic, Google "People Also Ask", and competitor FAQ analysis. Map high-volume questions to each program.

Week 2-3
3

FAQ Generation (AI-Assisted)

Use the templates + collected data to generate draft FAQs. AI can draft, humans review. Ensure each Quest has 15-20 AEO-optimized questions across all 6 categories.

Week 3-5
4

Expert Review & Approval

Quest managers and subject matter experts review FAQs for accuracy. Teachers may want to review answers about their methodology. Legal reviews claims and disclaimers.

Week 5-6
5

Schema Implementation

Add JSON-LD FAQPage schema to each Quest sales page. Add microdata attributes to FAQ HTML. Validate with Google Rich Results Test.

Week 6-7
6

Deployment & Monitoring

Deploy to production. Monitor Google Search Console for FAQ rich results. Track support ticket volume changes. Iterate based on new questions.

Week 7+ Ongoing
