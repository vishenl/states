Positioning Shift: From Energy to Brain Health

The most significant strategic change

Critical Impact
"
"I kind of look at it like a hybrid between a daily supplement, like an athletic greens... I take it for overall brain health... even if I wasn't doing a cognitively demanding task."
— Shawn Wells, FOCUS Creator

What We Changed

Before: Badge: "Caffeine-Free Energy"
Tagline: Focus on energy replacement
→
After: Badge: "Daily Brain Health"
Tagline: "Enter the flow state. Peak performance that feels effortless."

Why This Matters

Shawn explicitly positions FOCUS as a daily brain health supplement, not just an energy replacement. This fundamentally changes the value proposition:

  • Higher perceived value: Brain health > energy boost
  • Subscription justification: Daily use = recurring purchase
  • Differentiation: Separates from crowded "energy" market
  • Long-term thinking: Appeals to health-conscious customers

Conversion Principle Applied

Value Positioning: Customers pay more for health outcomes than for temporary energy. By positioning as "brain health," we elevate the perceived value and justify the premium price point.
